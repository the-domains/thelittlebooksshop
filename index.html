Not enough Filipino film buffs

Even as the Filipino indie film trend continues to make waves locally and internationally, many of its best productions have a hard time reaching their intended audience, which includes mainstream viewers. The big cineplexes tend to be leery of indies because, with only a few exceptions, they aren't "commercial" or accessible enough for many people to view and enjoy.
